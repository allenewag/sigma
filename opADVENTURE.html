The Story of the Straw Hat Pirates

The Straw Hat Pirates, also known as the Mugiwara Pirates, are an infamous and powerful pirate crew that originated from the East Blue. They are the main focus and protagonists of the manga and anime One Piece, led by the main protagonist, Monkey D. Luffy.

The "Straw Hats" are named after Luffy's signature straw hat, given to him by "Red-Haired" Shanks, and formerly owned by Gol D. Roger. They first sailed on the Going Merry, and later on the Thousand Sunny, built by Franky and Iceburg.

After being separated for two years, they reunited and have since become a major force in the New World, greatly disrupting the balance of the Three Great Powers. Following the Raid on Onigashima and the defeats of Kaidou and Big Mom, their captain Luffy officially became one of the new Four Emperors.

Straw Hat Crew

Monkey D. Luffy

The captain of the Straw Hat Pirates and the main protagonist of One Piece. He ate the Gum-Gum Fruit, which turned his body into rubber. His dream is to become the Pirate King.

Roronoa Zoro

The swordsman of the Straw Hat Pirates. He uses the Three Sword Style and aims to become the world's greatest swordsman.

Nami

The navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates. She is an excellent cartographer and has the dream of drawing a map of the entire world.

The Yonko (Four Emperors)

Shanks

One of the Four Emperors of the sea, captain of the Red Hair Pirates, and Luffy's childhood idol. He is the one who inspired Luffy to become a pirate and gave him his iconic straw hat.
